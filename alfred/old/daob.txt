KEBOKEB --- Area where land ends and mangrove swamp begins. Delongelel a klechutem ma keburs.
KEBURS -- Mangrove swamp. Okebong, oreomel era delongelel a Klechutem ma daob.
TAOCH -- Man made mangrove channel. Rolel a mlai era daob era chelsel a keburs.
DEMETAOCH -- Natural narrow mangrove channel, not used for transportation. Mekekrei el taoch, diak el klou a usbechel.
LALOU -- Lalou a ikel chelirarou el rachel el meriou, ma lechub and raod el ulekebkabes era keburs
UET -- Uet is an area that comes after the mangrove trees and before the area where seaweeds and seagrass usually grows. This area is little deeper than where mangrove trees grows and where the seaweeds and grass are found. Usually have big rocks in it.
LEMAU -- Small, deep spot within shallow area inside reef. Mekekrei el toachel el urebet era kereker.
KEREKER -- Area of ocean good for fishing at low tide;shallow area inside reef. Chei elmo meched era chedil a chei.
KEREKER---Se el delongelel a uet me a dailechei el se el bo le meched a chei e ng mo meched el mo diak a daob er ngii.
CHIS -- ng chis at the end ra keburs when it meets kerker
DAILECHEI -- Delongelel a kereker ma chelmoll.
MELEKESOKEL-- Meched el ruebet era toachel. Small, shallow spot found within deep water area inside reef.
MELEKESOKL-- aike el meched el ngar er a delongelel a kereker me a chelmoll.

CHELMOLL - Chemechumel a chei el delongelel a debeaot ma omedeklel a daob 
OREALL -- Highest part of the reef which can be walked on at low tide.
DAIMECHESENGEL -- Shallow area in open sea. Chelmoll el mora ikrel.
